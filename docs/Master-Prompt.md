# HOF — Master Prompt for Stitch (Quick Mockup)

Use the prompt below in **Stitch** (or any AI design tool) to generate a mockup that follows the HOF Design System. Copy the entire **Prompt** section and paste it into Stitch’s text prompt.

---

## Prompt

```
Design a dark-mode landing page mockup for HOF (House of Fiesta), a nightclub and fraternity-style venue in Cancún, México. Target audience: 20–28 years old, college, spring break, frat culture.

Brand: Bold, tribal, irreverent, playful, dramatic. Brotherhood, ritual, initiation, aesthetic chaos.

Tagline (must appear): “Rush the night. Break the rules. Be HOF.”

Colors (use exactly):
- Primary (CTAs, accents, logo): #E30613 (HOF red)
- Background: #000000 or #0A0A0A (near-black)
- Surfaces/cards: #1A1A1A or #252525 (very dark gray)
- Secondary (highlights, VIP, badges): #FFA800 (gold/orange)
- Success/confirm: #3C9945 (green)
- Links/info: #069DE3 (blue)
- Body text / subtle UI: #E9E9E9 (light gray)

Typography:
- Headlines / hero: bold, condensed or impactful (e.g. Oswald, Bebas Neue). Large “HOF” or “House of Fiesta” as hero.
- Body and UI: clean sans-serif (e.g. Montserrat). Short, punchy copy.

Layout and content:
- Hero section with “HOF” or “House of Fiesta” and the tagline “Rush the night. Break the rules. Be HOF.”
- One or two short sections with phrases like “You’re one of us now.”, “Sweat. Glow. Repeat.”, “Ritual. Brotherhood. Chaos.”
- Primary CTA button: solid #E30613, white text (e.g. “Get in”, “Reserve”).
- Secondary button: outline only (#E30613 or #FFA800), transparent fill.
- Cards or blocks with dark surfaces (#1A1A1A / #252525), subtle borders, soft shadow. Optional small badges: gold (#FFA800) for “VIP”, red (#E30613) for “Hot”.
- Footer with “HOF · House of Fiesta · Cancún” and tagline.

Visual style:
- Dark, high-contrast, premium nightlife feel. No clutter. Optional: flat graphic elements (red cups, beer pong, simple shapes). Logo area left with clear space; do not distort or use colors outside the palette above.
- Smooth, premium feel: suggest subtle hover states (slight scale, brightness) and section entrances (fade + slight vertical movement). No heavy or slow motion.
```

Bold headlines (Oswald-style), body Montserrat. Hero + tagline, primary CTA red button, VIP/gold badges. Tribal, bold, premium nightlife. Short copy: “You’re one of us now.”, “Sweat. Glow. Repeat.”
